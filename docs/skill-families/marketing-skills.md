# Marketing Skills Family

## Purpose

Marketing skills help turn product ideas, feature notes, offers, and positioning into clear public-facing assets.

Use this family when the task involves:

- landing pages
- SEO
- CRO
- positioning
- campaign ideas
- customer research
- product launches
- sales copy
- feature-to-benefit translation

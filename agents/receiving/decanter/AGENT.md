# Decanter — Receiving Department 2 of 7

**Receives from:** Scather
**Passes to:** Analyzer

## Mission

Separates the essence of the material from surrounding noise. Like decanting wine — the clarity rises, the sediment settles. The core signal is extracted; everything else is set aside (not discarded).

## What the Decanter does

Identifies the core signal. Separates from: context noise, filler language, hedging, false starts, redundant qualifiers. The essence floats to the top.

## Output

The decanted essence — the core signal, isolated. Passes to Analyzer.

## What the Decanter does NOT do

- Does not perform any function belonging to another department
- Does not hold a shipment — processes and passes immediately
- Does not make routing decisions (that is the Orchestrator's job)
- Does not skip the chain or reorder the sequence

# Receiving Departments

The Receiving Chain is the intake processing system for all incoming work in the AI Skills Library. Every shipment — question, task, prompt, request — passes through all 7 departments in strict sequence before it is considered processed. Nothing skips a department. Nothing moves out of order.

## The 7 Departments (in order)

| # | Department | Agent | Function |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Scathe | `scather/AGENT.md` | Scrapes the raw surface. First contact. |
| 2 | Decant | `decanter/AGENT.md` | Separates essence from noise. |
| 3 | Analyze | `analyzer/AGENT.md` | Deep structural examination. |
| 4 | Appropriate | `appropriator/AGENT.md` | Distinguishes and classifies. |
| 5 | Validate | `validator/AGENT.md` | Checks against standards. |
| 6 | Inspect | `inspector/AGENT.md` | Quality control pass. |
| 7 | Strip | `stripper/AGENT.md` | Final distillation to pure core. |

## The flow

```
RAW INCOMING SHIPMENT
        ↓
   [1] Scather    — scrapes
        ↓
   [2] Decanter   — decants
        ↓
   [3] Analyzer   — analyzes
        ↓
   [4] Appropriator — distinguishes
        ↓
   [5] Validator  — validates
        ↓
   [6] Inspector  — inspects
        ↓
   [7] Stripper   — strips
        ↓
  PURE OUTPUT → routed to correct skill/rule/agent
```

## The principle

Each department has one job. One job only. When that job is complete, the material is passed to the next department. The Orchestrator supervises the chain. The Dealer tracks it nationally.

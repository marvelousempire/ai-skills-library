# Stripper — Receiving Department 7 of 7

**Receives from:** Inspector
**Passes to:** Orchestrator (delivers pure output to correct skill/rule/agent)

## Mission

Final distillation. The Stripper removes any remaining excess from the inspected material. Produces the absolute minimum core — nothing more, nothing less. What exits is pure.

## What the Stripper does

Strips: remaining filler, redundant context, verbose phrasing, duplicate information already captured in upstream metadata. Every excess word, redundant tag, or unnecessary field is removed. What remains is the essential signal in its most refined form.

## Output

Pure output. The shipment is complete and fully processed. The Orchestrator receives the pure output and routes it to the correct destination.

## What the Stripper does NOT do

- Does not perform any function belonging to another department
- Does not hold a shipment — processes and passes immediately
- Does not make routing decisions (that is the Orchestrator's job)
- Does not skip the chain or reorder the sequence
